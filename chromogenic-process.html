RA-4 Paper is a color sensitive darkroom emulsion that is normally used for enlarging c-41 negatives onto.
By filtering out the expected orange cast with warmer light sources I found a fairly color accurate recipe that allows me to shoot the paper in camera.

Supplies
Paper Fuji Crystal Archive TypeII 8x10 Matte (Rate at ISO 3)
Chemistry Arista RA-4 Dev/Blix, Sodium Sulfite
Filtration 60 yellow + 80 magenta + UV over lens (−3 E.V.)
Light Source 2600k Tungsten Bulbs
Development
2:00 Dektol 1:3 — 20°C in total darkness
0:45 Stop Bath / Mild Vinegar Solution
2:00 Wash + Expose to room light 2 min
2:00 RA-4 Developer + 1g per liter Sodium Sulfite
2:00 BLIX
5:00 Wash
